About Me

From Sketches to the Global Top 1%: A 16-Year Creative Journey

My story began with a pencil and a fascination for art, leading to a fine art course in 1999. The turning point came when a senior director suggested I transition to the digital realm. After being named Best Student during my Diploma in Graphics Design, my career became a journey of constant innovation. Today, I am a leading global digital product creator with over 16 years of experience, bridging artistic vision with technical execution.

Local Expertise and Professional Leadership
Beyond global markets, I have spent over a decade in Bangladesh holding key roles at reputable firms. I served as Creative Team Leader at Amin & Jahan Corporation Ltd., mastering corporate design and leading large-scale brand projects. I also contributed my expertise to Textile Focus, Shahela IT Ltd, and Invogue Multimedia Ltd. These roles involved managing complex requirements, from high-end magazine layouts and newspapers to comprehensive corporate branding and packaging.

Specialist in Licensed and Copyright-Free Products
In an industry filled with copies, I pride myself on creating original, copyright-free, and licensed products. Designing for global platforms requires technical knowledge and attention to detail far beyond standard services. Every asset I produce meets international standards for quality, ensuring businesses can use my work with full legal and creative confidence.

Marketplace Authority: Envato, Creative Market, and Beyond
My work is featured on the world's most rigorous marketplaces, including Envato (GraphicRiver) and Creative Market. These platforms are known for strict Quality Checks (QC) and demanding technical standards. Every file I submit passes intense scrutiny for design rules and layer organization. Mastering these challenges has established me as a trusted author on Adobe Stock, Freepik, and Creative Fabrica, where my resources are utilized by millions worldwide.

Future-Ready: AI-Powered Creativity
I stay ahead of the technological curve by integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) into my workflow to enhance productivity. My AI-assisted capabilities include AI Image Generation, Advanced Photo Manipulation, AI Video Production, Motion Graphics, and AI Audio/Music Composition for multimedia projects.
